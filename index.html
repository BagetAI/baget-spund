Product & Hygiene Operations

Sterile Swap-and-Go Mechanics

Our premium B2B logistics model runs on standard 20L stainless-steel slimline kegs, which connect immediately into your existing draft lines. If your cafe doesn't have an under-counter draft setup, Spund provides a compact, whisper-quiet countertop tap kit.

1

Monday Morning Swap

We deliver filled, Nitrogen-pressurized cold kegs directly to your bar and retrieve your empty ones. All runs optimized by high-density Berlin routing.

2

Perfect Microfoam, Every Pour

No separating fats, no grittiness. Our oat milk undergoes commercial-grade high-pressure homogenization so it foams flawlessly and maintains complex latte art.

3

Strict HACCP Sterile Protocol

All returned stainless steel kegs are cleaned using automated two-station craft brewery washing machines, including 80 degrees Celsius caustic washes, sterile steam, and high-purity Nitrogen purges.
